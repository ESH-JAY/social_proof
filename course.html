Course Overview

Are you excited by robots and coding? This course provides a hands-on introduction to programming and robotics, guiding you through building and coding robots from scratch. Whether you're a beginner or have some tech experience, you'll learn to design, code, and control robots that can sense and respond to their environment.


Through practical projects, you'll explore key concepts in coding, robotics systems, and automation. By the end, you'll have the skills to tackle your own robotics projects and understand the basics of autonomous systems.
